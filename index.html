Revolutionizing assisted reproduction through sperm biology

We developed a proprietary sperm biology platform to significantly increase the effectiveness of treatments, enhancing the fertilizing capacity of sperm through the induction of hyperactivated mobility, in samples that normally acquire zero or low levels of hyperarousal. The Fecundis technique not only has the potential to improve fertilization rates in conventional in vitro fertilization techniques and in intrauterine insemination, but improves the efficiency of all assisted reproduction techniques, since it improves the quality of the embryo.
