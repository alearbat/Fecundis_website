We are developing sperm-focused treatments to achieve better blastocysts and more pregnancies

What are the solutions that the clinic currently has? There are two types of procedures:

  • Those of low complexity, such as uterine insemination, which are simple and inexpensive, and have an efficiency of less than 10%;
  • Those of high complexity, such as in vitro fertilization, with efficiencies close to 50%, which have an average cost of U$S 6,000 per treatment.

Neither of these treatments aims to improve sperm quality.
